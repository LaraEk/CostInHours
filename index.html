CALCULATING COST IN HOURS

Cost in Hours

How is Cost in Hours calculated? Very simply, the cost of the item is divided by minimum wage: this is how many hours a person making minimum wage needs to work to afford that item. For example, at Minimum wage you have to work 4.43 hours to make $32.12, the amount needed to fill a 12-gal tank.
